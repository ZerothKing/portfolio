# Better Timestamp Management (BTM)

**Role:** Architect & lead implementation

**Summary:** Implemented a firm-wide standard for timestamp serialization/deserialization across APIs, databases, and external SDKs. Improved observability with Datadog and ELK dashboards and drove internal adoption.

**Highlights:**
- Built synchronous configuration update system and periodic caching to reduce inconsistencies
- Implemented Airflow DAGs for periodic S3 uploads, integrated monitoring/health endpoints
- Conducted training and office hours for adoption across teams and SDKs
- Enhanced observability with dashboards and alerts

**Tech:** Java, Spring, Datadog, ELK, Apache Airflow, AWS S3, Gradle
